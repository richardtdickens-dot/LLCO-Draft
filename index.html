Our Mission

At Lens & Letter Co., we celebrate the beauty, heart, and spirit of East Tennessee through personalized greeting cards. Each card captures local Knoxville charm and Great Smoky Mountain scenery.

What We Offer

Our online card shop features local landscape photography by founder Richard T. Dickens. Browse a curated gallery of images and order printed 5x7 cards made and packaged in East Tennessee.

Our Inspiration

Born from a love of hiking and photography shared along Tennessee’s trails, we blend artistry and emotion. We believe in slowing down and celebrating moments with something real—crafted with care.

About the Founder

Richard T. Dickens is a Knoxville-based project manager, hiker, and photographer with a passion for connecting people through personal, local, and heartfelt cards that share natural beauty.
